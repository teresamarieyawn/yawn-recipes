Béchamel Sauce or White Sauce

Ingredients

1 TBSP olive oil
2 TBSP flour
1 tsp salt
black pepper to taste


How to bring it all together

Mix the above ingredients then heat and stir until you get a paste.

Add 1 cup of milk.

Use hand held blender to mix the flour/oil mash into the milk so there are no lumps.

Cook over moderate heat, constantly stirring (gently).

When it comes to a boil, keep it at a gentle boil.

Continue stirring until the sauce thickens.

Remove from heat.

Continue stirring until it stops steaming.

Let it set a few more minutes and it will thicken up even more.
